About the Department

The Department of Computer Applications was established to meet the growing demand for qualified professionals in the field of Information Technology. Our mission is to provide a high-quality learning environment that prepares students for dynamic careers and lifelong learning in a rapidly changing technological landscape.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: To become a center of excellence in computer education and research, empowering students with the skills and ethical values to make significant contributions to the industry and society.

Mission: To offer a comprehensive curriculum that balances theoretical knowledge with practical skills, foster innovation and critical thinking, and collaborate with industry to provide students with relevant, real-world experience.
